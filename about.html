About Flag Frenzy

Flag Frenzy Football Camp is a fun, high-energy flag football program for kids aged 8–14. We provide a positive environment where players of all abilities can learn, improve, and fall in love with the game.

What We Offer

- Non-contact flag football for boys and girls
- Skill-building drills and mini games
- Confidence-boosting activities and teamwork
- Positive, inclusive coaching style

Who Can Join

- Kids aged 8–14
- No experience necessary
- Multiple weeks and flexible scheduling
- Perfect for beginners or experienced players
