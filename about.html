About Flag Frenzy Football

We’re a London-based programme introducing young people to non‑contact American flag football. Our goal is simple: make sport a joy while teaching smart movement, teamwork and game IQ.

Who we are

Flag Frenzy Football Ltd is led by passionate, DBS‑checked coaches with experience in youth sport and safeguarding. We run inclusive sessions for ages 7–15, focusing on confidence, athletic development and positive team culture.

  • Robust safeguarding and clear behaviour standards
  • Game‑based learning that keeps players engaged
  • Progress you can see across fundamentals and decision‑making

What is flag football?

Flag football is a fast, non‑contact version of American football. Instead of tackling, players wear belts with flags — pull a flag to stop the play. That means no heavy pads, lower risk of injury, and more focus on speed, skill and strategy.

  • Non‑contact: no tackling or blocking
  • Builds agility, coordination and teamwork
  • Accessible for beginners; challenging for advanced players
  • Great crossover sport for football, rugby and athletics
