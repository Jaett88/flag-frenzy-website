About Flag Frenzy Football

Flag Frenzy was created with one mission: to make flag football fun, inclusive, and accessible for children of all backgrounds. We focus on developing confidence, teamwork, and love for the game — without the contact of traditional tackle football.

Why Flag Football?

Flag football is one of the fastest-growing youth sports. It’s fast, fun, safe, and doesn’t require expensive equipment. Players learn the fundamentals of football, communication, and teamwork — all while having a blast.

Our Commitment to Safety

All our staff are DBS checked and trained in safeguarding and first aid. We take the safety of every child seriously and maintain a safe, welcoming environment for players and parents alike.
