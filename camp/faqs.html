Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What age groups can attend?

Flag Frenzy is for children aged 7–15. We group players by age and ability to ensure everyone has fun and stays safe.

❓ What should my child bring?

Please bring water, comfortable sports clothing, trainers, and any necessary medical items (e.g. inhalers). We’ll provide the flag belts and equipment.

❓ What if it rains?

Sessions will go ahead in light rain. In severe weather, we’ll pause for safety or move to an alternative activity plan, and notify parents via the contact details provided at registration.
