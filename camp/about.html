About Flag Frenzy Football Camp

Flag Frenzy Football Camp is a fun, high‑energy flag football programme for ages 7–15. We create a positive, inclusive environment where players of all abilities learn, improve and fall in love with the game.

What we offer

  • Non‑contact flag football for boys and girls
  • Skill‑building drills and mini games
  • Confidence‑boosting activities and teamwork
  • Positive, inclusive coaching style

Who can join

  • Ages 7–15
  • No experience necessary
  • Flexible day or full‑week options
  • Great for beginners and experienced players
