Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What age groups can attend?

Flag Frenzy is for children aged 8 to 14. We group players by age and ability to ensure everyone has fun and stays safe.

❓ What should my child bring?

Please bring water, comfortable sports clothing, trainers, and any necessary medical items (e.g. inhalers). We'll provide the flag belts and equipment.
